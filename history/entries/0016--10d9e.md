## Approach

Two related SSE/APM improvements:
1. **Fourth APM/SSE stage keyed on match length** — calibrates the final probability by the current order-6 match length (low 8 bits) combined with whether the order-10 and order-12 match models are also active (1024 contexts). This 'match SSE' is most valuable inside long repeats (repetitive / structured / source data).
2. **Retuned the APM blend chain** — the first three APM stages were each blended at 75% toward the APM output (p = (p + 3a) >> 2), a ratio tuned when the chain had only two stages. With four stages that compounds into over-correction. Softening the first three to a 50% blend (p = (p + a) >> 1), and keeping the new match-length stage gentle at 25% (p = (3p + a) >> 2), is markedly better across every file.
APM tables are ~1 MB total, so memory and the CI footprint are unchanged from the record. Release and debug round-trip gates pass.
