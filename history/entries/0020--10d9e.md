## Approach

Adds eleven 4-sample strided context models — each hashes bytes at pos-k, pos-2k, pos-3k, pos-4k for k = 2..12. These extend the existing 3-sample strided bank to a longer periodic window, capturing record/columnar/aligned structure over four samples instead of three. All are byte-offset patterns with no corpus-specific content; each carries its own adaptive counter and bit-history StateMap into the layer-1 mixers.
