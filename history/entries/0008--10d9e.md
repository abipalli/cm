## Approach

Extends the context-model bank from 17 to 23 models — all general-purpose, no corpus-specific logic.
- **order-5**: contiguous order-5 hash (c4 + byte at pos-5), filling the gap between the existing order-4 and order-6 contexts.
- **order-11**: extends the direct-context ladder past order-9 (c4 + bytes pos-5..pos-11); previously only the match models reached beyond order-9.
- **Four sparse models**: a stride-2 sample (bytes pos-2/-4/-6) and three gap bigrams (pos-1 paired with pos-3, pos-4, pos-5). These capture interleaved / columnar / delimiter-shifted structure that contiguous contexts miss.
Each new model carries its own adaptive counter and bit-history StateMap into the mixer (NINPUT 39 -> 51). With more inputs the mixer is more confident per step, so the weight-update rate is retuned from 3/32768 to 5/65536 (~1/13107).
