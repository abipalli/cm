## Approach

Adds word-level n-gram context models, targeting natural-language text where the new brotli/zpaq/lpaq baselines show our biggest deficit (text_dickens trailed zpaq by ~1776 bytes).
The encoder already builds a rolling `wordhash` for the current alphanumeric run but discarded it at each word boundary. Now the last two completed words are retained (`prevword`, `prevword2`) and feed three new contexts:
- **Word bigram**: previous word + the word currently being typed.
- **Previous word + recent literal bytes**: models the punctuation/whitespace gap after a word and the run-up into the next one.
- **Word trigram**: the two preceding words + the current word.
Each new model carries its own adaptive counter and bit-history StateMap into the mixer. All are general language models — no corpus-specific logic. text_dickens improved 96325 -> 95735 (closing most of the gap to zpaq's 94549); struct_xml and source_samba also improved. Release + debug round-trip gates pass.
