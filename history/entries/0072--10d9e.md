## Approach

Completes the mixer fusion by applying it to the 10 layer-2 combiners; the previous PRs fused the 27 layer-1 mixers. All 10 layer-2 combiners dot the same 27-element layer-1-logit vector under their own selected weight row, so the dot and the weight update each load every input once and fan out to all 10 mixers, instead of 10 separate 27-wide loops. The per-combiner selected ctx, cached pr, weight updates, and the final logit-average are all unchanged, so the compressed output is byte-for-byte identical: SCORE 572577 record, all six per-file sizes unchanged; WORK 7.64G to 7.52G on scripts/measure-complexity.sh; round-trip suite passes. Because both mixer layers are now fused, the per-mixer Mixer mix/update methods have no callers and are removed, along with the now-unread n field. Raw pointers index the 10 distinct non-overlapping never-resized weight buffers for the update.
